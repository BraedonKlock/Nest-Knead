At Fluffy Butt Hut, our eggs are collected daily from well-cared-for hens raised right here on the farm. Never treated with chemicals or preservatives, our eggs are rich in flavor, vibrant in color, and packed with natural nutrients. Whether you're baking sourdough, flipping pancakes, or just cracking one open for breakfast, you'll taste the difference that true farm-fresh quality makes.
